Welcome to vRAF

Welcome to vRAF, a community built for aviation enthusiasts who love connecting, flying, and sharing their passion for virtual skies. Our mission is to create a fun, friendly, and safe environment for pilots of all skill levels.

Whether you’re here to improve your skills, make new friends, or just enjoy flying for fun, vRAF has a place for you. Join us today and be part of our skies as we take off to the next level!

Roadmap Milestone

The Path to VATSIM

vRAF is growing steadily, and we have exciting plans ahead! Once we reach 50+ members, we’re transitioning to VATSIM, opening the doors to a more realistic online flying experience, advanced air traffic control, and global connectivity.

This milestone marks a big step for our community, and we’re thrilled to bring our members along for the journey.
